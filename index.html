Looking for a scenic wedding location? Mackie Lake House was voted as one of the best locations to get married in the North Okanagan by Okanagan Life readers. Our wedding ceremonies are held on the expansive lawn overlooking Kalamalka Lake, framed by two very impressive and old Linden trees. The grounds provide many possibilities for wedding photos the challenge will be choosing the location. Mackie Lake House and Grounds is a provincially designated Heritage site and so you will receive guidelines to ensure it is as beautiful when you leave as when you came.

The Mackie Lake House is open year round and rents the House for meetings, seminars, small receptions and events.  Private tours and teas for groups of six or more are available by appointment year round or in conjunction with your event. The unique and comfortable atmosphere of the main floor of the Mackie Lake House offers an enjoyable meeting or workshop space for your business or club with wifi and kitchen facilities onsite.  It provides an excellent space to celebrate special events such as birthdays, retirements, or anniversaries.  Consider the Mackie Lake House for a memorable meeting or event!

This home epitomizes the standard of country living idealized by expatriate Britons living throughout the British Empire in the early twentieth century, and is the most physically and contextually intact example of the type of genteel orchard homes that were erected on this former part of Coldstream Ranch at that time.
